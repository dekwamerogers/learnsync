Reading this chart

Each bar shows the total number of unique learners who were active on that day of the week, counted across all historical data within the current filter scope.

A learner is counted once per day they had activity regardless of how many assignments they accessed or submitted.

Weekday bars are blue, weekend bars are grey. Use this to understand engagement rhythms and inform when to schedule nudges, reminders, and live sessions.
