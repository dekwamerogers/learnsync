Weekly Activity last 13 weeks · enrolled and activated counts are paid learners only · interventions include all learners
Enrolled / Activated / Graduated / Active / At Risk
All five are cohort-based: every number counts learners whose effective start date falls in that week. Enrolled = new enrolments. Activated = of those, how many have since passed their first module. Graduated, Active, At Risk = current standing of that same cohort today. Because all five use the same enrolment cohort, none of them will ever exceed Enrolled.
Interventions
Event-based: interventions logged during that calendar week, from any cohort. This is a measure of team outreach activity — it is not tied to an enrolment cohort.
